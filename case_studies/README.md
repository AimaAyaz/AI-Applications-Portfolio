# Case Studies

This folder includes all industry-focused case studies completed in ITAI 2372. Each report analyzes how AI is used in a specific sector, the benefits, challenges, and ethical considerations.

Included case studies:
- AGI in Healthcare
- AI in Manufacturing (Siemens + Waste Reduction Proposal)
- AI in Government & Public Sector (South Korea COVID-19)
- AI in Finance (Danske Bank Fraud Detection)
- Ethical Implications of Social Media Algorithms
- AI in Retail
- AI and Blockchain
- AI in Smart Cities
- AI in Healthcare & Agriculture
